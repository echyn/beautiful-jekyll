Updates:

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics at the University of Virginia (UVA) and a Faculty Research Fellow at the Rhode Island Innovative Policy Lab (RIIPL) at Brown University. My primary research fields are labor, urban and public economics. In my recent work, I have studied the effects of government programs on children. I received my PhD in Economics from the University of Michigan in 2016.
